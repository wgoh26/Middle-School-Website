What are you in for!

In middle school you will learn various things as you grow as a person. Who knows what you journey is going to be like, but it will most definitely have a lot of ups and downs.

Middle School Core Subjects

❮
Mathematics
In middle school, you will extend your knowledge on math from all that you have learnt in elementary school from algebra to geometry.
Science
In middle school science, you will explore the world through the eyes of scientists. You will explore multiple different subjects such as organs in the body, basic physics principles, the basics on how differnt elements react, and much much more!
Humanities
In middle school humanities, you will touch on many subjects on geography and history. You will learn many things that happened in the past that has influences the world we live in today, and you will also learn about the way the environment works and affects the way people live.
English
In middle school English, you will learn how to hown your language skills to write many masterpieces. You will do many book analyses, write many types of essays, and learn how to give excelent speeches.
